Help

Elk's stash uses a change algorithm to divide numerical values (could be exact gold amounts or scaled for your game's economy) into a collection of items of equivalent value.

Buttons

Exact

The amount you enter will be the exact value of the items generated, for when you know exactly how much value something contains.

Ballpark

A number between zero and the number you entered will be chosen, for when you have a rough idea of how much value something contains.

Choose For Me

A completely random value is chosen, for when you have no idea how much value something contains.

Item Tiers

Tier 1 and 2 (Value: 500 and Value: 200)

Items of very high value, often something made of rare materials, rare gems, or an exquisite feat of craftsmanship, found in tombs or in the possession of powerful enemies

Tier 3 and 4 (Value: 100 and Value: 50)

The prized posessions of the rich.

Tier 5 (Value: 10)

Everyday items that require a bit more craftsmanship, or slightly less common gem stones, found in similar places to tier 6 but much less commonly.

Tier 6 (Value: 5)

Cheap stones and common every day items, might be found in the pocket of a bandit or in a house.
